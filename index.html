The Spodcast

What is The Spodcast?

The Spodcast was a weekly(ish) podcast featuring Jared Earle, Eliot Earle (Spadge) and Stuart Dallas (Stut) discussing a wide range of topics from tech to TV to gaming to motorcycle racing, and everything in-between. It ran from August 14th, 2005 to November 27th, 2007.

The Spodcast is no longer produced, but every episode is archived here for posterity.
